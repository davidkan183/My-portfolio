About Me

A life lived
through art

I'm Euonia — an artist working across painting, photography, and mixed media. My practice is rooted in the belief that every surface holds a story waiting to be uncovered.

Trained in the classical tradition but drawn to contemporary expression, I move between disciplines freely, letting the work dictate its own form. I'm fascinated by impermanence — how moments, textures, and light exist only briefly before they change forever.

My studio is my sanctuary, my archive, and my laboratory. Every piece I create is an honest attempt to make the invisible, visible.
